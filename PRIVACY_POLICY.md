**Last Updated:** January 14, 2026

## Overview

Coding Learning ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only** using Android SharedPreferences:

- **Learning Progress**: Levels completed, current level, highest completed level
- **Performance Data**: Coins earned, stars earned per level, total stars
- **User Preferences**: Selected programming language
- **Problem Status**: Which coding problems you have completed
- **Session Data**: Temporary data during app usage (cleared on app close)

**Important:** This data is stored only on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers or any third party.

- ‚ùå Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- ‚ùå Location data
- ‚ùå Device identifiers for tracking
- ‚ùå Photos, contacts, or other sensitive data
- ‚ùå Browsing history

## Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

### 1. Piston API (Code Execution Service)

- **Purpose**: Execute user-written code (Python, Java, JavaScript, Kotlin, C++)
- **Data Sent**: Only the code you write and choose to execute
- **Data Retention**: Code is executed and immediately discarded, not stored
- **Website**: https://github.com/engineer-man/piston
- **Privacy**: No personal data is collected or stored

### 2. Google AdMob (Advertising)

- **Purpose**: Display banner ads, rewarded ads, and interstitial ads
- **Data Collected**: May collect advertising ID and app usage for personalized ads
- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- **Your Control**: You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings

### 3. Firebase (Analytics & Crash Reporting) - Optional

If enabled by the developer:
- **Purpose**: App analytics and crash reporting for improvement
- **Data Collected**: Anonymous usage statistics, crash logs
- **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **No Personal Data**: Only aggregated, anonymous data
